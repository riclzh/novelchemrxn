PI Richmond Lee's Bio
---
![Pic](https://scholars.uow.edu.au/file/n402666/LKC_+%28296%292.jpg){: width="200px"}

Richmond completed his PhD at the Australian National University under Australian Laureate Fellow [Prof. Michelle Coote](https://rsc.anu.edu.au/~mcoote/). After PhD in 2016, he embarked on a three-year independent fellowship at the Singapore University of Technology and Design, and subsequently moved to the University of Wollongong in Autumn 2020. He is hosted by [Yu](https://haiboyugroup.github.io/) and [Hyland](https://scholars.uow.edu.au/display/chris_hyland) groups.
